📌 Daily Rules (Always Apply)

  • 💧 Minimum 3–4 litres of water per day
  • 🍋 2–3 lemons with salt & pepper (lemonades) allowed per day. If skipping, take Vitamin C supplement.
  • 🥥 Cook in cold-pressed coconut oil or ghee (small qty). No fried items.
  • 🍫 80% dark chocolate max 15 g if craving chocolate · 1–2 dates if craving sweets after meal
  • 🥗 If hungry any time: eat green veggies + drink water
  • 💊 1 tbsp Psyllium husk (Isabgol) before bed only if constipated
  • ☕ Drinks: black coffee / black tea / herbal tea / diet drinks / water. No milk, sugar, sugary drinks.
  • 🦷 Thyroid: Use fluoride-free toothpaste. Dandelion tea + Aloe vera juice weekly (twice).
  • 🫙 Aloe vera juice recipe: 200g aloe pulp + 5g sea kelp powder + 5g spirulina powder + 200ml tender coconut/milk + honey/zero sugar 2 tsp
  • 🌿 Free spices: turmeric, cinnamon, ginger, dill, parsley, chili flakes, paprika, cayenne, coriander, cumin, basil, rosemary, oregano, thyme, cardamom, nettle leaf, fennel seeds, psyllium, bay leaf, fenugreek
  • 🏃 Weekly: 200 min exercise · Brisk walk 10 min after meals · Castor oil pack (Follicular/Ovulatory phases)
  • ⚠️ Fish disliked — skip fish items. Any non-veg curry = chicken/prawn/mutton preference.
